Polling Place Locator

Online Help

The Polling Place Locator application helps voters locate their assigned election polling place (regular or advanced polls), get driving directions, comment on the polling place conditions, and obtain basic candidate information.

Using the Application

To begin, a voter enters their address, selects a point on the map, or uses their current position. The application will then find the assigned polling place and highlight it along with the polling division area. The route from the search address or point to the polling place will also be displayed. In addition, information about that polling place and candidates will be displayed in tabs at the bottom of the application.

Regular (Election Day) or Advanced Polls Selection

The application starts with Regular (voting day) polling places selected. The voter can locate advanced polls by selecting the Display Advanced Polls icon. The voter can switch back to regular polls by selecting the same icon.

Address Search

  1. Enter an address in the search bar
  2. Press enter to search immediately or select a result from the autocomplete list.
Those using a mobile phone or older browser will need to select the Search icon
to display the search field.

My Location Search

The voter can select the Geolocation icon
to conduct a search based on their current location.

Polling Place and Candidate Information

The Polling Place tab contains the following:

  • Directions to the polling place
  • The name of the polling place and its address
  • The hours of operation and any specific accommodations
  • Voter registration deadlines and the election date
  • Contact information

The Candidates tab displays the names of candidates for all offices in your district.

Map Functions

  • Use the basemap selector
    (not available on smartphones) to display your preferred basemap.
  • Select + or – in the upper left of the map to zoom in or out.

Print

The print function
next to tabs prints the information in the Polling Place tab along with the map. This function is not available on smartphones.

Submit Comments about a Polling Place

  1. Click on a polling place symbol on the map to display the polling place information popup.
  2. Click on the Comments icon
    in the popup to input and submit a comment. You will also see comments that have been submitted by other voters.

Share Your Map

You can click on the Share icon
to share your map in an email, a Facebook post or on Twitter.

Browser Compatibility

The Polling Place Locator is supported on the following browsers: Microsoft Internet Explorer 7/8/9/10/11, Mozilla Firefox, Google Chrome, Apple Safari, iOS Safari 4.0+, and Android Chrome 2.3+ and most modern JavaScript enabled browsers.
